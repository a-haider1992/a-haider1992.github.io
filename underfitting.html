Deep Learning Best Practices

Underfitting in Deep Learning Models

Underfitting occurs when a deep learning model is too simple to capture the underlying patterns in the data. This results in a model that performs poorly on both the training data and unseen data.

Causes of Underfitting

  • Models that are too simple
  • Insufficient training time
  • Too much regularization

Preventing Underfitting

  • Use more complex models
  • Train for more epochs
  • Reduce regularization
  • Ensure sufficient feature engineering

Conclusion

Underfitting is a common challenge in deep learning, but with the right techniques, it can be mitigated. By understanding the causes and implementing strategies to prevent it, you can build models that capture the underlying patterns in the data.
